Negotiating power for buyers

Instant negotiating power, on the spot. Know your preferred RRP and work backwards to the cost price you need — or simply negotiate your price with your supplier, with your goal margin already worked out. Walk in knowing your number.

Import with ease

If you know your container costs, you can create a precise calculation — FOB price, conversion rate, customs duty, agency fees, cartons, weights and quantities. If your supplier can quote quantity per carton with its weight and size, you're on your way to the best imported landed calculation.

Cost your goods locally too

Buying local is great! Cost your products quickly whilst with your supplier — be decisive and place your order on the spot. Local freight, quantity, purchase price in; landed cost and RRP out.

Do your distribution centres

Use third parties to stock and distribute your products? Work out your margin with a seller taking commission or a DC in the chain — and see your true landed cost per unit including freight, CBM and volumetric weight.

Save, organise & share

Save calculations into folders with photos and notes. Export any calculation as CSV or PDF, or share a whole folder as a ZIP — straight to email, Drive or your team.

Enjoy your sales

Have a clearer mind with indicative margins and landed prices. Negotiate, then recheck your costs once invoices with exact pricing are in hand. All that's left is to enjoy your sales — knowing you've bought well.
