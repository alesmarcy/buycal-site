Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026 · Applies to the BuyCal Android app and this website.

The short version

BuyCal does not require an account and does not collect, sell or share your personal information. Your calculations stay on your device. The only personal data we ever receive is your email address, and only if you choose to give it to us.

Your data stays on your device

Calculations, folders, notes and photos you save in the BuyCal app are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded to us or to any third party. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Email newsletter (optional)

In the app and on this website you can optionally share your email address to receive tips and product updates. If you do, your email is sent to MailerLite, our newsletter provider, and used only for that newsletter. You must confirm your subscription by email (double opt-in), and every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Declining or skipping this has no effect on the app.

Photos

If you attach a photo to a calculation, BuyCal uses your device's photo picker. The photo is stored only with your calculation, on your device.

Currency rates

The Currency Check feature requests live exchange rates from open.er-api.com. This request contains no personal data.

Sharing and exports

When you export a calculation (CSV, PDF or ZIP), you choose which app receives the file via your device's share sheet. BuyCal does not send exports anywhere on its own.

Data deletion

App data: uninstall the app, or clear its storage in Android settings. Newsletter: click unsubscribe in any email, or contact us and we will remove you.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@buycal.com.au
